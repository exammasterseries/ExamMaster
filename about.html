FAQ ⚡
How do I join ExamMaster?▼
Click the "Join — ₦1,600" button anywhere on the page. Enter your name and email in the secure payment popup, then complete your Paystack payment. You'll get immediate access.
Which subjects are covered?▼
ExamMaster currently covers PHY102 (General Physics II), CHM102 (General Chemistry II), and MTH102 (General Mathematics II) — the three core 100-level science courses.
How does the scoring work?▼
Every correct answer earns you 10+ points. Each day has 25+ questions (max 100 points). Over 5 days, the maximum total is 3500+ points. Leaderboard rankings are based on cumulative score.
When does the leaderboard reset?▼
The weekly leaderboard resets every Monday at 00:00. Season rankings accumulate across all weeks. Weekly top performers receive recognition; season champions are announced at season end.
What are the rewards?▼
Top performers each week receive special rewards and public recognition. Season champions receive premium prizes. Referral bonuses are also available for students who bring in classmates.
Can I retry a quiz if I scored badly?▼
Every battle is a "One-Shot" event. Your first-attempt score is final and is the only one sent to the Live Leaderboard. However, the season is a marathon, not a sprint! If you miss a mark, the next daily quiz is your opportunity to recover points and climb back to the top.
